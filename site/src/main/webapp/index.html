Welcome to Carolina Pet Company

The vision of quality, commitment and passion are the driving forces that ultimately became Carolina Pet Company.

Our attention to detail, style and practicality for the discriminating pet owner are the formula for the unique and quality pieces that are welcome additions to any home décor. Now your pet can have the same style and comfort that you do.

Manufactured in the heart of the textile industrial corridor of South Carolina we combine domestic production with a global supply chain to bring American pets, products made by American workers.

When only the best will do, the answer is Carolina Pet. Look for the CPC pet logo and know you are getting a quality product for your family pet.
